We may be the little red dot, but did you know that five countries that sent teams to Russia this year actually have a smaller population than we do? Not having a horse in the race - or a team in the Cup - has not stopped them from going crazy for favorites like England, Brazil, Spain, and South Korea.

Five teams competing in Russia are smaller than Singapore.


Two of them have made it to the quarterfinals.
Do you know which ones?

Singaporeans are consuming World Cup matches at an alarming rate at home, at bars, and community centers nationwide. A spokesperson from People’s Association said that when all is said and done, they expect a 600,000 viewers to watch matches at community centers nationwide. The most popular ones are Tampines and Keat Hong locations.
